TikTok Reviewer Guide

Purpose

Single-user, read-only productivity integration for organizing and analyzing the authenticated user’s own saved TikTok links.

Requested access

  • user.info.basic
  • portability.all.single
  • portability.all.ongoing

Data flow

  1. User authorizes TikTok.
  2. Jarvis uses localhost callback, PKCE, and one-time state.
  3. Portability data is requested after approval.
  4. The archive is processed in an isolated temporary directory.
  5. Only Favourite Videos saved date and landing-page link are retained.
  6. Unrelated categories are discarded and the raw archive is deleted.
  7. Analysis remains local and creates candidate tasks only.

Unsupported actions

No posting, uploading, messaging, DM reading, commenting, liking, following, profile editing, purchasing, ads, livestream management, or account changes.
