Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

This policy explains how Stirty processes information through Jarvis TikTok Intelligence.

Information processed

  • Basic TikTok account identity information authorized through Login Kit.
  • Saved/favourite video date and landing-page link obtained through an authorized portability flow or user import.
  • Optional notes, categories, analysis results, review status, and audit metadata.

Information not intentionally retained

Direct messages, purchases, payment information, contacts, login history, search history, unrelated archive data, passwords, browser cookies, and session tokens are not intentionally retained.

Purpose

To connect the user’s own TikTok account, organize saved-video links, analyze their relevance, detect duplicates, and prepare review-only candidate tasks.

Storage and sharing

Operational data is stored locally on the user’s computer. Data is not sold, used for targeted advertising, or provided to data brokers.

Portability archives

Archives are processed in a temporary isolated directory. Only Favourite Videos information is retained. Unrelated categories are discarded and the temporary raw archive is deleted after successful extraction.

Retention, deletion, and revocation

The user can delete imported items, delete analysis records, clear retained TikTok intelligence data, or revoke the TikTok connection.

Security

Jarvis uses localhost callbacks, PKCE, one-time state, bounded archive extraction, authentication, and audit controls. No system can guarantee absolute security.

User rights

Depending on applicable law, users may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or withdrawal of consent. Contact jarvishelpyourself@gmail.com.
